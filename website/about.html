About SHIFT IN PARADIGMS

Our journey, mission, and vision for a more awakened world

Sonia's Story

SHIFT IN PARADIGMS began as my personal journey of awakening and transformation. After experiencing profound shifts in consciousness and perception, I recognised the need for accessible, practical guidance for others seeking to break free from limiting belief systems and societal conditioning.

What started as a small blog sharing my personal insights has grown into a comprehensive platform offering courses, books, and community support for those on the path of spiritual sovereignty and conscious evolution.

Today, SHIFT IN PARADIGMS serves as a beacon for truth-seekers around the world, providing the tools, knowledge, and community support needed to recognise and transcend the matrix of illusion that shapes our perceived reality.

Our Mission

At SHIFT IN PARADIGMS, our mission is to empower individuals to recognise and transcend the invisible systems of control that limit human potential. We believe that true freedom begins with awareness, and that each person who awakens to their sovereign nature creates ripples of transformation throughout the collective consciousness.

We are committed to providing practical, accessible tools and knowledge that bridge ancient wisdom and modern understanding, helping you to:

  • Recognise the nature of reality constructs and how they shape perception
  • Clear unwanted energetic attachments and limiting belief patterns
  • Develop heightened awareness and expanded consciousness
  • Reclaim your inherent power as a sovereign being
  • Create a life aligned with your authentic self and highest potential

Our Values

Truth & Authenticity

We are committed to sharing knowledge and insights with integrity, honesty, and transparency, even when challenging conventional narratives.

Empowerment

We believe in providing tools and knowledge that empower individuals to become self-sufficient in their spiritual journey, rather than creating dependency.

Sovereignty

We honour each person's inherent right to self-determination and freedom from external control systems, supporting the reclamation of personal power.

Compassion

We approach all teachings and community interactions with deep compassion, recognising that awakening can be both liberating and challenging.

Community

We foster a supportive environment where seekers can connect, share experiences, and grow together on their journey beyond the matrix.

Evolution

We embrace continuous growth and evolution, both personally and collectively, as we expand our understanding of consciousness and reality.

Meet Sonia Regina

Sonia Regina

Founder & Spiritual Guide

Sonia's journey of awakening began through profound personal experiences that shattered her perception of conventional reality. With years of dedicated study in consciousness exploration, energy work, and ancient wisdom traditions, she brings a unique blend of practical knowledge and spiritual insight to her teachings.

Through her own transformation and liberation from limiting belief systems, Sonia recognised the urgent need for accessible, practical guidance for others seeking to break free from the matrix of societal conditioning and step into their sovereign power.

Today, Sonia dedicates her life to sharing the tools, techniques, and wisdom that have transformed her own existence, helping others recognise their true nature and reclaim their inherent freedom.
