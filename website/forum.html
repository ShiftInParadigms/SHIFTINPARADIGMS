Community Guidelines

Our community is built on mutual respect, support, and a shared commitment to growth and awakening. To maintain this space as a sanctuary for authentic exploration, we ask all members to adhere to these guidelines:

  • Respect: Treat all community members with respect, even when perspectives differ.
  • Authenticity: Share from your own experience and speak your truth with compassion.
  • Support: Offer encouragement and constructive feedback to fellow seekers.
  • Privacy: Respect the confidentiality of personal experiences shared in the forum.
  • Sovereignty: Honor each person's unique path and right to self-determination.

By participating in our community, you agree to uphold these principles and contribute to a space of growth, healing, and transformation.
